Frequently Asked Questions

Clear operational guidelines to support our distribution workflows.

Where are distribution products sold?
We position and manage inventory distribution networks across premium, high-volume digital marketplaces and strategic enterprise e-commerce portals in North America.
Does Zar Ventures Inc. utilize drop-shipping structures?
Absolutely not. We function on asset-owned corporate retail frameworks. We pay upfront, allocate full inventory financing, hold stock inside dedicated fulfillment nodes, and absorb sales risk completely.
How are market valuation discrepancies and pricing issues handled?
Our systematic scanning assets trace multi-channel indexes around the clock. If unauthorized listings destabilize market valuations, our internal reporting alerts partner channels directly to execute clean market compliance tracking.
What are your standard payment and purchasing terms?
We typically open accounts on standard commercial terms or wire payments immediately upon invoice approval. Our priority is to streamline transactions to meet your standard accounting and warehouse dispatch pipelines quickly.
How quickly do you review and place orders on new catalogs?
Our internal inventory procurement system evaluates price list arrays, compliance guidelines, and logistics data quickly. Initial purchase processing options are normally issued back within 24 to 48 business hours.
